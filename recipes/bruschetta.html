Bruschetta

Description

Bruschetta (pronounced broo·skeh·tuh) is a classic Italian appetizer that brings the freshest flavors of summer together! Using fresh ingredients like tomatoes, basil, garlic and olive oil, this is the best bruschetta recipe you will find!

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes – roma taste best!
  • Basil leaves
  • Garlic (about 9 cloves) – chopped (not pressed)
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Parmesan cheese – shredded
  • Kosher salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Extra virin olive oil
  • Melba toast (or bagel chips)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, toss in the tomatoes, basil, garlic, and parmesan cheese, until each bite has a sufficient amount of each. Add more if necessary.
  2. Sprinkle in salt and pepper to taste, red wine vinegar, and stir. Add olive oil. The mixture should be nice and shiny. Let it sit in the refrigerator for about an hour to allow the flavors to mingle.
  3. Either use as a dip or place the bruschetta on top of your toast and enjoy with some balsamic glaze!
